# Creative & Marketing Services (301-350)

This catalog details AI-native microservices focused on Marketing, Content Creation, and Design.

## 301. Blog Post Generator
* **Description**: Generates comprehensive, SEO-optimized blog posts from a simple outline or topic.
* **Features**:
  * Keyword injection.
  * Structure formatting (H1, H2, lists).
  * Tone customization.
* **Requirements**:
  * **Models**: GPT-4, Claude 3.
* **UI Flow**:
  1. Input Topic: "Benefits of AI in Healthcare".
  2. Select Length: "Long form (1500 words)".
  3. Output: Full HTML blog post.
